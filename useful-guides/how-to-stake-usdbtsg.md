# How to Stake $BTSG

1. Go to your BitSong Wallet at [_wallet.bitsong.io_](https://wallet.bitsong.io) and Log in.&#x20;
2. On your wallet Click on "**Validators**", in the left Men√π.\

3. Then click on "**Stake"**.\
